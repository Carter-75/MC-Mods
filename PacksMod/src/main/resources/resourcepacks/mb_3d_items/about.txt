 -=- MB-3D Items Pack -=-
https://www.planetminecraft.com/texture-pack/mb3d-items-pack/

This is a resource pack that attempts to remodel Minecraft's item sprites into full 3D models that still fit within the blocky style of Minecraft.
I've seen a few other resource packs / mods that tried to do the same thing, but none of them had remade every item in the game, and fewer of them did so in an art style fitting that of Minecraft. So, here we are.

Compatible with the Placeable Items mod! https://www.curseforge.com/minecraft/mc-mods/placeable-items
Compatible with the Vivecraft mod! (use the included patch) https://modrinth.com/mod/vivecraft
